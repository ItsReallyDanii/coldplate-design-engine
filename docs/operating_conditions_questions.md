# Operating Conditions Questions

Checklist of unresolved operating-condition items to answer from literature and specifications.

- [ ] Coolant type: Which fluids are assumed; what purity and additive requirements apply?
- [ ] Target Reynolds regime: Expected laminar, transitional, or turbulent range; sensitivity to local porosity.
- [ ] Heat-flux range: Junction or wall heat-flux envelopes to cover, including hotspot excursions.
- [ ] Chip footprint assumptions: Die size, heat-source map, inlet/outlet placement constraints.
- [ ] Inlet temperature assumptions: Coolant supply temperature range and stability requirements.
- [ ] Allowable pressure drop range: System-level budget and per-cold-plate allocation.
- [ ] Single-phase vs two-phase scope: Whether two-phase operation is included or explicitly excluded.
- [ ] Conjugate heat transfer requirements: When solid conduction must be co-modeled with fluid domains.
- [ ] Transient vs steady-state scope: Required transient coverage (startup, load steps) versus steady-state only.
