# Manufacturing Constraints Notes

## Minimum feature size
- Document smallest printable channel width, lattice strut thickness, and hole diameter per process.

## Wall thickness
- Record recommended wall thicknesses for pressure containment and thermal performance; note process-dependent limits.

## Trapped volume / powder evacuation
- Identify features prone to powder entrapment or trapped volumes and the mitigation approach (drain holes, breakup channels, removable inserts).

## Internal channel accessibility
- Note requirements for cleaning, inspection, and deburring; capture any minimum bend radius or straight-length rules for tooling or flow.

## Printable metals / processes to track
- List candidate alloys and processes (e.g., L-PBF, binder jetting, CNC) with constraints on surface finish and dimensional tolerance.

## Post-processing issues
- Capture heat treatment, HIP, support removal, surface finishing, and sealing steps that impact internal geometry or tolerances.

## Geometry rules to promote to hard constraints
- Track rules that should become design constraints (e.g., overhang angles, aspect ratios, fillet radii, minimum ligament between channels).
