# Source Capture Template

## Citation
- Authors, title, venue, year, DOI or URL.

## Why this source matters
- Brief statement of the contribution or constraint it informs.

## Exact metrics reported
- List the metrics, units, reference planes, and any normalization details exactly as stated.

## Geometry / baseline details
- Geometry class, baseline used for comparison, boundary conditions relevant to the geometry.

## Solver / experiment details
- Solver type or experimental setup, turbulence/porous models, meshing or instrumentation notes, and validation steps reported.

## Manufacturability details
- Process used or assumed, feature sizes, surface finish, post-processing, and any stated limitations.

## What can be reused in this repo
- Data, correlations, boundary-condition definitions, or modeling practices directly reusable here.

## Unresolved questions
- Items requiring follow-up or clarification from the text, supplementary materials, or related work.

## Stage applicability
- Whether the source informs Stage 1, 2, 3, 4, or 5; include brief justification.
